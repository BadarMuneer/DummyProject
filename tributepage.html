Dr Israr
Ahmed (R.A)
An Scholar with a lion Heart

Early Life and Education:

Israr Ahmed was born in Hisar, in British Indian Empire, on 26 April 1932 After graduating from a local high school in Sahiwal Ahmed moved to Lahore Join King Edward Medical University in 1950. In the same year he joined Jamaat-e-Islami led by Abul Ala Maududi,After recieving his MBBS degree in 1954 he started practicing medicine. Later on in 1957 he resigned from jamaat-e-islami due to its involment in national politics.

Work:

He criticised modern democracy and prevalent elctoral system and argued that the head of an islamic state could reject the majority decisions of an elected assembly. He criticised the values of west, and talked about Israel or conspiracies by jews without anyfear. manymore, he talked about the Jihad, Islamic values, Social System of Islam, Econonmic system of Islam his main idea was revival of Khilafah Ahmed was Awarded the Sitara-i-Imtiaz in 1981.
